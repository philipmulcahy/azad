The offer...

(Scroll down for more.)

This extension is developed and maintained by volunteers, who give their time and expertise for free. In exchange, we ask for charitable donations to Princess Alice Hospice, a UK facility for end of life palliative care. In recent months, there has been significant demand for "commercial" type features, that are of particular relevance to users who have very large order histories, typically those who are using this extension to assist their business activities. Philip set out a charitable donation total target before he would release some new "commercial" features. The total has increased, but not as fast as expected. This page offers you a short cut to those features (and future capabilities that are similarly held back).

If you subscribe, you are making a charitable donation and as a side-benefit you will get access to all the preview features as-is, for the subscription period. You are not buying entitlement to creation of new features, bug fixes, other code changes, consulting, design work, or refunds.

Cancellation and refunds

The subscription may autorenew unless you cancel before the renewal date by unsubscribing. If you don't like what you get, then you should not renew (e.g. unsubscribe). Failure to cancel by the renewal date shall not be grounds for refund.

Chargeback

Chargeback attempts (whether successful or not) expose me, and thus the charity to significant costs in time and money. As explained in the cancellation section above by subscribing, you are making a non-refundable donation, but yet a small proportion of users don't seem to understand this, or don't care. Chargeback attempts may be met by: 1) the public sharing of your identity so as to prevent future fraud, and 2) permanent disablement of the free features in the extension for your user account.

Fees

Small fees may charged by up to three intermediaries meaning that the Princess Alice Hospice will receive slightly less than your payment:

  • ExtPay (how this extension connects you to Stripe)
  • Stripe (credit card and Foreign Exchange)
  • JustGiving (because I will transfer takings to the hospice via them)
I (Philip) do not benefit from any of these charges.

Feedback

Please consider that I am a single, part-time volunteer when contacting me about problems. While Stripe has my contact details, if over-entitled users behave as if I work for them, attempt to call me, or ask for refunds, I might be forced to shut down the project and sell it to a commercial concern, who will likely monetize the extension more aggressively than I am minded to. The right way to seek help is to participate in discussions on the Github project or the Chrome extension's support page.
